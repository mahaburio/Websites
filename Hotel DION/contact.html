How to get to Hotel Dion

the bus

Take Hexin, Alaha, Guoguang, Feigou, Zunlong, Tonglian and other passenger vehicles across the province, get off at Chaoma Station in Taichung, and walk for about one minute from Chaoma Station to Dinglong International Business Hotel.

drive by yourself

Get off Taichung Shalu Interchange (178km) on National Highway No. 1, turn around at the Henan Intersection and take the slow lane, go straight for 100 meters to reach Dinglong International Business Hotel, get off Taichung Longjing Interchange on National Highway No. 3 (182km), and walk from Zhongxing Road Turn to Taiwan Boulevard, drive on Taiwan Boulevard towards the city for about 15 minutes, turn around at Henan intersection and take the slow lane, go straight for 100 meters and you will arrive at Dinglong International Business Hotel.

train

From the whole province, take the Taiwan Railway to Taichung Railway Station, then take the bus 5.27.48.88.106.147 and get off at "Chaoyang Bridge (Autumn Red Valley) Station, and walk for half a minute to Dinglong International Business Hotel.

high speed rail

Take the high-speed rail across the province to Taichung Wuri Station, take a taxi for about 12 minutes or take the high-speed rail shuttle bus to Chaoma Station, and walk for about 1 minute to Dinglong International Business Hotel.

Airport

Taichung Ching Chuan Kang Airport, after arriving at the station, it is recommended to transfer to Passenger Bus No. 150 (about 90 minutes one way), get off at Chaoma Station, and walk for about 2 minutes.Taoyuan Airport, after arriving at the station, it is recommended to take the Flying Dog Bus, Kuo-Kuang Bus, and Tonglian Bus and get off at Taichung Chaoma Station. You can walk for 2 minutes to reach Dinglong International Business Hotel. The one-way journey time is about 150 minutes.
